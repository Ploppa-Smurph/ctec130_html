Meet Big Boyce

Our founder and head chef, Big Boyce, puts his heart into every batch of brownies.

With a passion for baking that started in his grandmother’s kitchen, Boyce has perfected the art of creating oversized, chocolatey masterpieces that are a hit with everyone who tries them. He believes in using only the finest ingredients to ensure that every bite is a delicious experience.
